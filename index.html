Then & Now Gallery

This is a demo template for the Then & Now photo gallery app we made for Spokesman.com. We're big fans of The New York Times Then & Now photo widget, so we made a Django+jQuery app for our site. You can see it in use here.

You can use the j/k or ←/→ keys to navigate up and down this page. Use the sliders below a picture to show before/after.
